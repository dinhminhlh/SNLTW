Founded in Herzogenaurach, Bavaria by Dassler brothers in 1924. The main products of the Adidas brand include shoes, clothes, hats, socks, sports bags ... The advantage of Adidas brand products is that they are always made from the best materials, designed quite carefully. Meticulous, careful, delicate shown in each seam to bring users a comfortable and pleasant feeling. Today, Adidas is the second largest sportswear company in the world. Its products are now present in 160 countries and annually launch more than 660 million products to serve the needs of users.
